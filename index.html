About Me

My name is Triston Tetley and I am a full stack web developer. Without hesitation I'd say my favorite part of web development is the problem solving. That being said my passion is working with the back-end, because the front-end seems to have less freedom when it comes to how you make something work. While, in the back-end ten people can all create functions that make a program run. Yet, each and every one can be unique in it's own way. So I am taking small yet meaningful strides to become a master of the mighty JavaScript everyday!
